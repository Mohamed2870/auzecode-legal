Privacy Policy

Effective September 13, 2025

Who we are

AuzeCS is an interview practice app. Contact: auzecode@outlook.com.

Data we collect

  • App data you provide: display name, answers in interview sessions.
  • Microphone audio to run mock interviews. (Permission is requested by iOS.)
  • Transcripts of your answers to create feedback.
  • Purchase info for credits via Apple (StoreKit). We do not receive your full payment details.
  • Optional analytics if you enable “Share anonymous usage analytics”.
  • AI processing: Your answers in interview sessions are sent to OpenAI’s ChatGPT API to generate feedback. We do not share personal info beyond what is needed for this service.

How we use data

  • Run interviews, create transcripts and summaries.
  • Provide and improve AuzeCS, prevent abuse, and support you.
  • Process purchases and maintain your credit balance.
  • Send text and audio to OpenAI’s ChatGPT API to provide AI feedback in interview sessions.

Processing & storage

Audio is recorded locally on your device. If you use features that send audio/text to our servers for transcription or review, the files are transmitted securely and deleted when no longer needed to provide results. Transcripts and session summaries may be stored on device; you can export or delete them inside the app.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing occurs with service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, error reporting) strictly to operate the service. Limited sharing occurs with OpenAI’s API strictly to provide AI-based feedback.

Children

AuzeCS is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the app if you are under the minimum age in your country.

Your choices

  • Microphone permission can be changed in iOS Settings.
  • Toggle analytics and tracking in the app’s Privacy & Legal screen.
  • Export or delete local data in Settings → Privacy & Legal.
  • Request server‑side deletion by emailing auzecode@outlook.com.

Security

We use industry‑standard security measures. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will change the “Effective” date above and, when appropriate, notify you in‑app.

Contact

Email auzecode@outlook.com for privacy questions or requests.
